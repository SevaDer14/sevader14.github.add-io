What is Weyl Semimetal?

Weyl semimetal is a crystal with specific conducting properties that originate from quantum interactions between electrons and crystal lattice. Those interactions influence electrons that take part in conductivity to become massless quasiparticles - Weyl fermions. Conductivity with such particles has number of benefits that allow for apllications in spintronics and future computing.

My Research in Physics

While I was employed in Institute of Metal Physics of Russian Academy of Science I was involved in galvanomagnetic study of HgSe single crystals doped with small amounts of transition metal impurities such as Fe, Co and Ni. During the study we observed anomalus galvanomagnetic effects such as extreemley large and linear magnetoresistance, negative longitudinal magnetoresistance and large quantum oscillations in Hall effect. During our research we established that probable explanation for observed effects can be formation of non-centrocymmetric Weyl semimetal phase in our samples.
