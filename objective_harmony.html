The Goal

The YouTube channel is about exploring the origin of consonance and dissonance from psychophysiological perspective. I refer to it as fundamental music theory. The goal is to come up with approaches and software to control tension in music by smart manipulation of partials of sound. That will allow for new artistic effects and may break new ground in musical composition.
