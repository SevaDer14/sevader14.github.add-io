The Goal

The YouTube channel is dedicated towards exploring the origin of consonance and dissonance from psychophysiological perspective. I refer to it as hardcore music theory. The goal is to came up with approaches and software to controll tension in music by smart manipulation of partials within a sound for new artistic effects. and break new ground in musical composition.
